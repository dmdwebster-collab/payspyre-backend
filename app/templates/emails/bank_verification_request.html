Important: please do NOT manually add bank account numbers, as this may conflict with the automated verification process.

What the bank verification does NOT provide:

  • We do NOT receive your online banking login or password information
  • We canNOT create or modify information or transactions

What the bank verification does provide:

  • Account owner(s) information (name, email, phone number, and address)
  • Account information (institution name and number, account name, account type, and account and routing numbers)
  • Account transaction information (amount, date, type, and description)
  • Account balance information (current and available balance)
  • Credit account(s) information (due dates and balance owed, payment amounts and dates, transaction history, and interest)
  • Loan account(s) information (due dates, balances, payment amounts and dates, interest, loan type, payment plan, and term)

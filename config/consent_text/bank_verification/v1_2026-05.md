# Bank Account Verification Consent

**What this covers.** You consent to PaySpyre and its banking-data partners securely connecting to, or otherwise verifying, your financial accounts to confirm account ownership, balances, and income or transaction history relevant to your application.

**What data is collected.** Depending on the verification method, this may include your account and institution numbers, account holder name, account balances, and a history of deposits and transactions. Where you link an account through a third-party banking-data provider, that provider transmits this information to us on your behalf.

**How the information is used.** We use this information to confirm that the account belongs to you, to assess your income and ability to repay, and to detect fraud. We do not sell this information and we do not store your online banking credentials.

**How long we keep it.** We retain verification results and the financial data used in your assessment for as long as your account or application is active and for the period required by applicable Canadian law, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

**Your right to withdraw.** Providing this consent is voluntary, but without it we may be unable to verify your income or finalize your application. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us and, where applicable, by disconnecting the linked account. Withdrawal does not affect processing already completed.

# Hard Credit Check Consent

**What this covers.** You consent to PaySpyre obtaining a "hard" credit inquiry about you from one or more Canadian credit reporting agencies (such as Equifax or TransUnion). A hard inquiry is performed when you proceed with a formal credit application.

**What data is collected.** The credit reporting agency may share your full credit report, including your credit score, credit accounts, balances, payment history, public records, and prior inquiries.

**Impact on your credit.** Unlike a soft inquiry, a hard inquiry is recorded on your credit file and may be visible to other lenders. It can have a small, temporary effect on your credit score.

**How the information is used.** We use this information to make a credit decision, to determine the terms we can offer, and to meet our legal and risk-management obligations. We do not sell your credit data.

**How long we keep it.** We retain your credit report and the decision based on it for as long as your account or application is active and for the retention period required by applicable Canadian law, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

**Your right to withdraw.** This consent is voluntary, but without it we cannot complete a formal credit decision. You may withdraw it at any time by contacting us; withdrawal does not reverse an inquiry already made, and we may retain records we are legally required to keep.

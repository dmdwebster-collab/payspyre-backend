# Hard Credit Check Consent

I consent to PaySpyre Inc. ("PaySpyre") obtaining a full credit report about me from a consumer reporting agency (credit bureau) in connection with my application for financing (a "hard" inquiry).

I understand that:

- A hard inquiry **may affect my credit score** and may be visible to other lenders.
- This inquiry is made because I am proceeding with a financing application, and the report is used to make a credit decision.
- The information is collected, used, and disclosed in accordance with the applicable provincial consumer-reporting legislation, PIPEDA, and PaySpyre's Privacy Policy.
- I have the right to obtain a copy of my credit report directly from the credit bureau, including if my application is declined.
- I may withdraw this consent at any time before the inquiry is made, subject to legal and contractual restrictions, but doing so may prevent PaySpyre from making a credit decision on my application.

By checking this box, I confirm I have read and agree to this Hard Credit Check Consent.

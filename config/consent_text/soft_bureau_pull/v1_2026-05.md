# Soft Credit Check Consent

**What this covers.** You consent to PaySpyre obtaining a "soft" credit inquiry about you from one or more Canadian credit reporting agencies (such as Equifax or TransUnion). A soft inquiry lets us review your credit profile to estimate whether you may pre-qualify for a credit product.

**What data is collected.** The credit reporting agency may share your credit score, credit history, outstanding balances, payment history, and similar information held in your credit file.

**Impact on your credit.** A soft inquiry is visible only to you and does **not** affect your credit score. It is different from a hard inquiry (see the separate hard credit check consent).

**How the information is used.** We use this information only to assess your eligibility and to present pre-qualified options. We do not sell your credit data.

**How long we keep it.** We retain the results of the soft inquiry for as long as your account or application is active and for the retention period required by applicable Canadian law, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

**Your right to withdraw.** This consent is voluntary, but without it we cannot estimate your eligibility. You may withdraw it at any time by contacting us. Withdrawal does not undo inquiries already performed, and we may retain records we are legally required to keep.

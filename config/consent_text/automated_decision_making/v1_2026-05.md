# Automated Decision-Making Consent

**What this covers.** You consent to PaySpyre using automated processing — including rules-based logic and statistical or scoring models — to evaluate your application and to make or support decisions about whether to offer you credit and on what terms.

**What data is used.** The automated process may use the information you provide, your verified identity and bank data, your credit report, and information derived from these sources (such as calculated income, debt-to-income ratios, and risk scores).

**How decisions are made.** Automated processing helps us deliver fast, consistent decisions. A decision may be made solely by automated means. Significant adverse decisions are subject to the safeguards required by applicable law.

**Your rights.** You have the right to request information about the main factors that affected an automated decision, to request human review of a decision made solely by automated means, and to express your point of view or contest the outcome. Where a decision results in adverse action, you will receive the notice required by applicable law.

**How long we keep it.** We retain the inputs and outcomes of automated decisions for as long as your account or application is active and for the retention period required by applicable Canadian law, after which records are securely deleted or anonymized.

**Your right to withdraw.** This consent is voluntary, but without it we may be unable to process your application efficiently or at all. You may withdraw it at any time by contacting us; withdrawal does not affect decisions already made.

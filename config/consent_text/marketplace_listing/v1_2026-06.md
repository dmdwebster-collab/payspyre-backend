**Marketplace listing consent**

By publishing a listing on the PaySpyre treatment marketplace, you agree that
PaySpyre may share a **de-identified** summary of your treatment need with
participating clinics so they can express interest in providing your care.

Before you select a clinic, clinics will only see information that does **not**
identify you — for example: the type of treatment you need, how soon you need it,
the first three characters of your postal code (your general area), and a general
indication of your financing readiness. Clinics will **not** see your name, full
address, email, phone number, or any government identifier.

Your name and contact details are shared with a clinic **only after you choose to
select that clinic**. You may pause or close your listing at any time.

This consent is specific to the marketplace. It does not affect your financing
application or any other consent you have given. You can withdraw it by closing
your listing.

# Identity Verification Consent

We need to confirm that you are who you say you are. This is a legal requirement before we can offer or service any credit product.

**What this covers.** You consent to PaySpyre and its identity-verification partners collecting and processing the personal information needed to verify your identity. This may include your full legal name, date of birth, residential address, and government-issued photo identification — including a scan or photograph of the document and, where applicable, a live selfie used to match your face to that document.

**How the information is used.** The information is used solely to verify your identity, to comply with applicable "know your customer" and anti-money-laundering obligations, and to protect against fraud and impersonation. We do not sell this information.

**How long we keep it.** We retain identity records for as long as your account is active and for the period afterward required by applicable Canadian law (currently a minimum of several years for regulated financial records). After that period, records are securely deleted or anonymized.

**Your right to withdraw.** Providing this consent is voluntary, but without it we cannot verify your identity and therefore cannot offer you credit. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us; withdrawal does not affect processing already carried out, and we may retain records we are legally required to keep.
